# SSA Disability Claims Case Study

## Research Question
Why do disability claim approval rates vary by up to 25 percentage points across US states?

## Key Finding
Poverty rate is the strongest predictor of approval rate disparities (r = -0.674, p < 0.001), explaining 45.5% of all variance nationally.

## Structure
- `ssa_analysis.py` — main data analysis
- `hypothesis_testing.py` — statistical hypothesis testing
- `data/` — master dataset and external variables
- `outputs/` — report, charts, Excel results

## Data Sources
- SSA State Agency Monthly Workload Data (2000-2026)
- U.S. Census ACS 2023
- Kaiser Family Foundation 2022
- Bureau of Labor Statistics 2023
